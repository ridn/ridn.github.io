About

Product-minded engineering: clear requirements, reliable releases, and software that teams can operate without heroics.

I lead engineering delivery across time zones—roadmaps, code review, release discipline, and the “last mile” with stakeholders when trade-offs matter. Most of my recent work sits on large enterprise SaaS stacks (think complex data models, compliance, and managed product shipping), but the through-line is general software practice: APIs, automation, measurable cost savings, and documentation people actually use.

I enjoy GTM-adjacent technical work too: release comms, enablement artifacts, security review prep, and turning fuzzy asks into scoped increments. I also use modern LLM-assisted tooling day to day to move faster on design, code, and test ideas—always with human review where it counts.

This site still hosts some earlier iOS and Cydia-era projects. For a résumé-style timeline and skills, see experience or the PDF résumé.
